Our Story

Built on Precision

Novel Strategic Communications (NSC) was founded on a singular conviction: that every high-level engagement deserves to be captured, communicated, and remembered with precision.

From policy forums and corporate summits to broadcast productions and NGO conferences, we bring professional communications expertise to every table we sit at.

Based in Tema, Ghana, our team combines award-winning journalism, broadcast experience, and strategic communications expertise to deliver outputs that truly matter.
